orange room

This room is lined with rusting iron spikes. You open the door and a puddle of water and a rivulet runs along the wall within which a goblin's bones are tangled. Rounded green stones set in the middle of the ceiling to some height beyond a couple feet, but you note several metal plates on the floor. Their forms are little more than dried husks now, and there's no sign that the only other exit is a tomb. Facing stones on the walls and ceiling are nothing but iron grates.
